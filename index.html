Features

AI Business Intelligence
Gemini AI analyses your orders, inventory, and buyer patterns. Get production forecasts and demand insights daily.
📱
WhatsApp at Every Step
Order confirmations, delivery OTPs, payment receipts, and balance reminders — all via WhatsApp automatically.
📍
GPS Geofencing
Every delivery is GPS-verified. Know if your agent actually reached the store. Full audit trail with location data.

Real-time Ledger

Automatic buyer zone detection from address. Zone-wise reports show top buyers, revenue, and delivery density.

8 Report Types

Production quantities flow directly to dispatch. Inventory auto-updated on every delivery. Zero manual entry.

From order to cash.
Fully automated.

2
Factory produces
AI forecasts quantities. Factory team enters production. Inventory updated automatically.
3
Agent delivers
Agent scans QR, GPS verified, OTP confirmed. Delivery recorded with items and amounts.
4
Payment collected
Cash, UPI, or online. Ledger updated. WA receipt sent to buyer. Balance always accurate.
