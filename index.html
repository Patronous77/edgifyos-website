How it works

From order to cash, fully automated

Four steps. Zero manual reconciliation.

1

Buyer orders

Buyer logs in and places an order. Admin notified via WhatsApp within seconds.

2

Factory produces

AI forecasts quantities. Factory enters production. Inventory updated automatically.

3

Agent delivers

QR scan, GPS verified, OTP confirmed. Delivery recorded with items and payment.

4

Payment reconciled

Cash, UPI, or online. Ledger updated. WhatsApp receipt sent. Balance always accurate.
