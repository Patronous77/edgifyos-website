How it works

Four steps. Zero
manual reconciliation.

1

Buyer orders

Buyer logs in and places an order. Admin notified instantly.

2

Factory produces

AI forecasts quantities. Factory enters production. Inventory updated automatically.

3

Agent delivers

QR scan, GPS verified, OTP confirmed. Delivery recorded with items and payment.

4

Payment reconciled

Cash, UPI, or online. Ledger updated. Receipt sent. Balance always accurate.
